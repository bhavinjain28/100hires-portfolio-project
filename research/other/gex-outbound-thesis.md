# Growth Engine X Outbound Thesis (Summary)

**Source:** Public Smartlead partner profile + podcast appearances (Eric Nowoslawski)  
**Collected:** 2026-06-20

## Three pillars

1. **Deliverability** — Smartlead/Instantly best practices at 1M+ emails/month; inbox placement before copy optimization.
2. **List building** — 20+ data providers; firmographic + technographic + trigger data (open jobs, funding, tech stack changes).
3. **Messaging** — 7-step product-message fit process; test multiple angles in week one; AI for relevance at scale.

## Key ideas for playbook

- Treat cold email like media buying: run 20–50 concurrent campaigns to test variables.
- No clean "B2B SaaS" filter—build TAM with multiple scrapers and accept 80% completeness early.
- Manual 10-minute research email → automate that same quality to the whole market via Clay workflows.
- LinkedIn and email should not be tightly orchestrated; email volume >> LinkedIn message volume.
