## 2025 — Outbound is alive (humans + AI)

- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/retentionadam_2018-outbound-is-dead-thanks-outreach-activity-7360006134600011778-UrEV
- **Collected:** 2026-06-20
- **Context:** Kyle's response on Adam Robinson's post

> Outbound is alive and well—as long as it's done right. That means: strong account research, great contact research, omni-channel systems, POV building. Most important: HUMANS executing. AI's role is equipping humans with what they need for high quantity of high quality outreach.

**Why collected:** Enterprise marketing leader view on AI-assisted outbound without replacing human execution.

## 2024 — Personalized messaging guide

- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/guide-personalized-messaging-kyle-coleman
