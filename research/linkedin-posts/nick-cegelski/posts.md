## 2024: 3x3 rule breakdown

- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/nick-cegelski_3x3-rule-for-cold-emails-1-frontload-what-activity-7101968424486019072-FtMs
- **Collected:** 2026-06-20

> 3x3 rule for cold emails:
> 1. Frontload what you know about them
> 2. Explain in a single sentence the problem you solve (save other "value" for the meeting)
> 3. Low friction CTA
>
> Three bodies of text, no scroll on phone.

**Why collected:** Enterprise AE perspective on concise cold email; pairs with Armand's operator framing.
