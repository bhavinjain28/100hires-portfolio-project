## 2024 — The 3x3 rule for cold email

- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/armand-farrokh_30mpc-sales-sdr-activity-6854096838383943681-Ggbt
- **Collected:** 2026-06-20

> The 3x3 rule. A cold email must not be longer than 3 bodies of text. No longer than 3 lines each. Content flow:
> 🟢 **Problem:** Dangerously specific ("crispy" per Josh Braun)
> 🟢 **Solution:** How you solve that in one sentence, max
> 🟢 **Ask:** Low friction—"open to taking a peek?"
>
> Avoid: boring general problems, feature dumps, asking for 30-minute meetings or suggesting times.

**Why collected:** Mobile-first email structure from an operator who scaled Pave to $13M ARR.
