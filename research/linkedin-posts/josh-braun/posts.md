## 2025: 4T framework walkthrough

- **URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/josh-braun_lets-write-a-good-cold-email-using-the-4t-activity-7307052447791796224-AXSH
- **Collected:** 2026-06-20

> Let's write a good cold email using the 4T framework:
> 1. **Trigger** — Why this person vs someone else?
> 2. **Think** — Neutral question about a potential problem.
> 3. **Third-party credibility** — Proof others solved it.
> 4. **Talk?** — Low-pressure yes/no question.
>
> Yes, you can steal the template—but it's the "why" or underlying psychology that helps you apply it.

**Why collected:** Most cited modern cold email structure; anti-salesy tone aligns with skeptical B2B buyers.
